GYM AS A HOBBY

Ever heard of a pump? Think of it as you pumping air into a balloon but in this context you are pumping blood into your muscles, Isn't that just cool? Most people assume gyming is only about health but actually it has more benefits to it and a lot of interesting stuff you can learn.

LET'S TALK GYM BENEFITS

Ever since I joined the gym, I have taken note of a list of benefits that have made my life a whole lot better than before. I have noted that Gyming improves:

  • Body Strength
  • Energy levels
  • cognition
  • Self-confidence
  • Stress management
  • Mebotalism

GYM TEACHINGS

The key lessons I have learned about having gym as a hobby are : discipline, patiance and resiliance. All those lessons come from those days where you feel weak and like life is not worth living but still push yourself for progress sake. Gym is even recommended by therapists and psychologists because it has been proven to be therapy on its own.
